What is Fuliza?

Fuliza is an overdraft service provided by NCBA Bank via Mpesa. When you don't have enough money in your MPESA account, you can use this service to complete MPESA transactions such as sending money and paying bills.

In Need of a Higher Fuliza Limit?

Every three months, Safaricom Fuliza limits are reviewed. Frequent use of the service may increase your limit, but only after three months. Some emergencies, however, may not be able to wait that long.
